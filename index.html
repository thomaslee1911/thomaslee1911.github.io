Thomas Lee


I'm a Senior Trading Analyst at BETM (part of Mitsubishi Corporation), focused on proprietary trading in nodal electricity markets, as well as energy storage optimization. Previously, I researched electricity markets at Penn's Kleinman Center for Energy Policy, and researched quantitative investment strategies at AQR Capital. I graduated from Penn's interdisciplinary M&T Program, with bachelor's degrees in Economics and Computer Science, and an MS in Electrical Engineering. I'm interested in energy decarbonization issues at the intersection of engineering and economics, including interactions between policy and private investment.
