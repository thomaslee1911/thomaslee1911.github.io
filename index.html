Thomas Lee



Hi! I'm currently a Senior Trading Analyst at Boston Energy Trading and Marketing (part of DGC, Mitsubishi Corporation), where my work now focuses on proprietary trading in nodal electricity markets and structured transactions valuation. I also worked on BETM's energy storage optimization and valuation platform, now used for a growing fleet of third-party battery assets in ISO markets.

Previously, I researched electricity markets at Penn's Kleinman Center for Energy Policy, and worked on quantitative investment strategies at AQR Capital. I graduated in 2017 from Penn's interdisciplinary M&T Program, with a BS in Economics, BAS in Computer Science, and MSE in Electrical Engineering. I'm generally interested in energy decarbonization issues at the intersection of engineering, economics, and policy. Please find some of my research-related work here.
